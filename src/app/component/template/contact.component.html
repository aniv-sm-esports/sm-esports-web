What is the purpose of this project?

The purpose of this project is to create a collaborative, people-focused, backbone for Super Metroid Esports. It will seek to promote the sharing of responsibility that comes with gig-economic labor - while attracting more people and investors to the group.

What are some of the goals?

There will, undoubtedly, be many creative minds that wish to participate! I'd expect that most people will want to have some way to communicate, firstly! So, here's a short list of things that come to mind.

  • (Board) Ownership / Collaborative daily-discussion / Sharing of roles
  • (Media Role) A group media front which would be a dedicated (shared) role to post daily / weekly / monthly (youtube based video) ads
  • (Promotion) Merch, Networking, and Business Reach-Out
  • (Legal) Copyright, Twitch, Youtube, and Paypal details to share with the board and new users
  • (Schedule / Events) What's going on this day / week / month? What group events do we have planned?

How can I get involved?

There's one role that comes to mind - developer! If you have software expertise, wish to learn software, or want to help out with testing, and documentation, this is where I get to win! ^_^ Please contact me, or see the project site to sponsor this group!
